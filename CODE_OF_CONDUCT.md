# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

AutoAppearance should be a welcoming project for people with different levels
of experience, backgrounds, identities, and perspectives.

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent while still naming problems clearly.
- Give constructive feedback that focuses on the work.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when needed, and move forward.

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces such as issues, pull requests, discussions,
and any other official communication channels for AutoAppearance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block
participants whose behavior is harmful to the project or community.

If you need to report a concern, contact the maintainers privately. Include
links, screenshots, or context when it is safe to do so. Reports will be handled
with care and only shared with people who need the information to respond.
